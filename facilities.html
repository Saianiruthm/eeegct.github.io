Facilities

Advanced Measurements Laboratory

Equipped with setups for measuring resistance, inductance, and capacitance using bridge circuits, sequence impedances of synchronous machines, and more. It also includes an instrumentation amplifier, a phase angle measurement kit, and a setup for data acquisition through virtual instrumentation.

Electronic Devices and Circuits

This lab features a digital spectrum analyzer, logic analyzers, waveform processors, oscilloscopes, measuring instruments, and analog discovery kits. It also includes a computer-based data acquisition system and a mixed-signal analysis workbench.

Digital Electronics Laboratory

Here, students can design logic and arithmetic circuits, registers, counters, encoders, decoders, and multiplexers/demultiplexers. The lab also includes a PAL/PLA implementation kit and allows for the programming of combinational and sequential logic circuits using HDL.

Microprocessor and Microcontroller Laboratory

This lab is equipped with an 8085 microprocessor kit, kits based on the ARM processor, a PIC microcontroller, and a setup for DSP-based speed control of a five-phase induction motor.

Computer Simulation Laboratory

Equipped with several software and hardware to train students through simulation. It includes PSPICE, LabVIEW, DigSILENT software, Xilinx Integrated Synthesis Environment Foundation Series, MATLAB – Simulink Software, Embest Integrated Development Environment for ARM and PIC Microcontroller, Code Blocks in C software, and SCILAB software.

Power System Laboratory

Features a three-phase fault analyzer, setups for differential protection of 3-phase transformers and generator protection, a generating station setup, a 400 kV HVAC transmission line model, a measurement and protection section, a receiving station setup, an RLC loading unit, a dynamic grid fault simulator, a SCADA-based transmission line analyzer, and a smart grid simulator.

Smart Grid Technology Laboratory

Equipped with a grid supply unit, AC transmission and distribution setup, generating station setups, a 3-phase solar grid-connected inverter, a 3-phase modular multilevel inverter, and more.

Power Electronics and Drives Laboratory

This lab includes kits for SCR, TRIAC, MOSFET, and IGBT, rectifier kits, a buck, boost and buck-boost converter kit, a single-phase PWM inverter kit, a series inverter kit, and a setup for BLDC motor speed control.

AC Machines Laboratory

Equipped with various induction motors, 3-phase synchronous motors, 3-phase alternators, a single-phase alternator, autotransformers, and single-phase and three-phase transformers.

Motor Testing Laboratory

Features motor test rigs with dynamometers and load sensors, an eddy current dynamometer and load cell, a power analyzer, motor testing software with a personal computer, a power supply unit, and more.

DC Machines Laboratory

Equipped with compound, series, and shunt motors, a DC generator (self and separately excited), and a resistive load setup.

Control Systems Laboratory

Features a digital position control system and a programmable logic controller experimental setup.

Centre of Excellence – Alternate Energy Research Laboratory

Equipped with a solar grid-tied training system, a solar PV emulator, a power quality analyzer, a weather monitoring station, a wind turbine emulator, a solar system analyzer, a smart grid simulator, a solar PV performance analyzer, and more.
